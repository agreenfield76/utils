Print Services billing preference

This is your status within the Print Services managed copy/print system.

Staff accounts should be marked as 'billable' to enable multiple charge codes or have a single billing code to just charge to that one account.

Student accounts should be non-billable to enable the use of personal balances.

Changes made here will be applied to the copy/print system overnight.

For further information, please contact the IT Service Desk.
