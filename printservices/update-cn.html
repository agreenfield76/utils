Common Name

Your common name is made up of your preferred name and your surname.

This attribute will be updated nightly with your preferred name and your surname. Only update this attribute for changes to take immediate effect.

To alter this attribute, enter your new details in the box provided.

Strings must be 2 - 32 characters ("a-z", "0-9", ".", "-"," ").

WARNING - use of this facility is strictly monitored.

To alter the preferred name edit preferred name on the form. Changes to preferred name should be automatic.
